Requirements & Upload

All required items and your upload area in one place.

  • Credit Application (completed and signed).
  • Bank Statements Form (last 3 months of business or personal statements).
  • Truck Purchase Agreement (dealer purchase order or bill of sale for the truck/trailer).
  • Copy of driver's license (and CDL if applicable).
  • Proof of insurance or agent contact (if already available).
  • Proof of legal status (Green Card, Work Permit, or Citizenship).
