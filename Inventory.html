How our inventory program works

We are not a public truck lot. In most cases, you will be buying from a dealer, fleet, or private seller of your choice. Our role is to evaluate the truck or trailer, confirm the value, and structure the financing.

If you already have a specific VIN or stock number, include it with your application so we can review mileage, condition, and route to make sure the numbers make sense for you.
